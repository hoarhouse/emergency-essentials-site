Critical 72-Hour Timeline

1H

Immediate Response

Assess situation, secure immediate area, establish communication with authorities and family members.

6H

Stabilization

Set up temporary shelter, ration supplies, monitor emergency broadcasts for status updates.

24H

Resource Management

Inventory remaining supplies, establish routines, coordinate with neighbors and relief efforts.

72H

Recovery Planning

Prepare for evacuation or extended stay, document damage, connect with relief services.
